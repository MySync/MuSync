Your Fave Songs & Lyrics

MuSync incorporated the YouTube API into MuSync, allowing you to easily search your favorite songs using the YouTube search engine. MuSync also incorporated the MusixMatch API to allow you to search for your favorite song lyrics while listening to it simultaneously.

User Experience Focused

MuSync allows you easily create and save playlists to your registered accounts. We did our best to design MuSync to be intuitive for you to use, by providing an easy to use and seamless user experience.

Current Limitations

Due to content uploaders placing usage/site/regional restrictions on their videos, you will not be able to view certain licensed videos. Unfortunately, this is a beta version of our app and we currently do not have the financial resources to purchase those licenses.
